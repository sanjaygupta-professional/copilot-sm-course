# Microsoft Copilot for Scrum Masters

A comprehensive, hands-on course teaching Scrum Masters to leverage Microsoft Copilot for team facilitation, ceremony management, and agile workflows.

## Course Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | ~5.5 hours |
| **Levels** | 3 (Foundation, Advanced, Visuals) |
| **Modules** | 14 total |
| **Target Audience** | Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches |
| **Prerequisites** | Microsoft 365 Copilot Enterprise license |

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:

- **Process ceremony notes** in minutes instead of hours
- **Synthesize retrospective feedback** into actionable themes
- **Analyze velocity and team metrics** with AI assistance
- **Facilitate ceremonies** with Copilot as a thinking partner
- **Track and escalate impediments** systematically
- **Coach team members** with prepared conversation guides
- **Create team visuals** for reviews and celebrations

## Course Structure

### Level 1: Foundation (~3.5 hours)
Core Copilot skills for Scrum Masters

- 1.1 Welcome to Microsoft Copilot (15 min)

- 1.3 Your First Scrum Master Tasks (30 min)

- 1.5 Persona Prompting (20 min)
- 1.6 Building Context with Memory (15 min)
- 1.7 Copilot Across M365 Apps (15 min)
- 1.8 Copilot in Microsoft Teams (25 min)
- 1.9 Copilot in Outlook (20 min)
- 1.10 SharePoint & Collaboration (20 min)

### Level 2: Advanced SM Workflows (~1.5 hours)
Complex Scrum Master tasks with Copilot

- 2.1 Facilitating Ceremonies with Copilot (35 min)
- 2.2 Impediment Management & Resolution (30 min)
- 2.3 Team Health & Continuous Improvement (30 min)

### Level 3: Team Visuals (~45 min)
Creating visuals with DALL-E and Designer

- 3.1 Image Generation Basics (20 min)
- 3.2 Scrum Master Visuals (25 min)

## Getting Started

1. **Verify Access**: Go to [copilot.microsoft.com](https://copilot.microsoft.com) and sign in with your M365 enterprise account
2. **Start with Module 1.1**: Open `lesson-modules/1-fundamentals/1.1-welcome/MODULE.md`
3. **Follow the exercises**: Each module includes hands-on exercises with sample prompts
4. **Use the sample files**: Upload files from `sample-files/` when exercises call for them
